Common Legal Terms in Rental Law

Tenant:
Person who rents property.

Landlord:
Owner of property.

Lease:
Agreement between tenant and landlord.

Security Deposit:
Money paid as guarantee.

Eviction:
Legal removal of tenant.

Notice Period:
Time before ending agreement.

Fair Rent:
Rent fixed by authority.

Subletting:
Tenant renting property to another person.
