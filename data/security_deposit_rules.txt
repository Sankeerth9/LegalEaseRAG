Security Deposit Rules in Telangana

- Security deposit is typically 2 to 3 months of rent.
- Deposit must be refunded after tenant vacates.
- Deductions allowed only for:
  - Property damage beyond normal wear and tear
  - Unpaid rent or utility bills

Landlord must:
- Provide explanation for deductions
- Return remaining amount within reasonable time

Unfair practices:
- Holding deposit without reason
- Excessive deductions
